Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install my printer?

You can install your printer by downloading the latest drivers and following the on-screen instructions.

Why is my printer not connecting to Wi-Fi?

This issue may occur due to weak signals or wrong Wi-Fi password. Reset your network settings and try again.

How do I fix paper jam issues?

Turn off your printer, remove stuck paper carefully, and clean the paper tray before restarting.

Why are my prints coming faded?

Check ink/toner levels and clean the print head to improve print quality.
